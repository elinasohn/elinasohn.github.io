Design Guide for Experimental Site

Site Purpose

The purpose of the experimental design is to showcase a design of my choice that demonstrates some interesting aspect of HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript coding or interactivity.

Target Audiences

People who are interested in my works, HR managers, and front-and-developers are the main target users. I put the interesting mini-game on the home page so that I could attract their attention as soon as they enter the site.
